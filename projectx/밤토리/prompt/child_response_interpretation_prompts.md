
# Child Response Interpretation Prompts

## Core Principle
Child responses are **signals**, not answers.

## Signal Types
- Silence → fatigue, processing, or avoidance
- Single word → low effort but positive engagement
- Laughter/Noise → playfulness or avoidance
- Mixed KR/EN → intent understood, language limited

## Interpretation Rules
- Assume positive intent by default.
- Never penalize brevity.
- Confirm meaning gently via Popo.
